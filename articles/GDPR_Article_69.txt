Art. 69 GDPRIndependence

The Board shall act independently when performing its tasks or exercising its powers pursuant to

Articles 70

and

71

.

Without prejudice to requests by the Commission referred to in

Article 70

(1) and (2), the Board shall, in the performance of its tasks or the exercise of its powers, neither seek nor take instructions from anybody.
