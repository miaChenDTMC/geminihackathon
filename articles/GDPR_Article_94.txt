Art. 94 GDPRRepeal of Directive 95/46/EC

Directive 95/46/EC

is repealed with effect from 25 May 2018.

1

References to the repealed Directive shall be construed as references to this Regulation.

2

References to the Working Party on the Protection of Individuals with regard to the Processing of Personal Data established by Article 29 of

Directive 95/46/EC

shall be construed as references to the European Data Protection Board established by this Regulation.
